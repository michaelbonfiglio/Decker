Computers

The Decker Library has workstations for use by students, faculty, and staff for research, internet, and email. The entire Library is wireless enabled. Printing in Color or B/W is available from any computer in the Library, though not from laptops brought by library users.

iMacs

On the upper level, there are Macs for quick email and internet use. On the lower level, in the Digital Classroom area, there are 18 iMac computers. All have the following software:

  • Adobe Acrobat Professional
  • Adobe CS4 (does not include InDesign, Illustrator, or Dreamweaver)
  • ARTstor Offline Image Viewer
  • CD and DVD burning
  • Firefox
  • iTunes
  • iLife
  • iWork
  • Microsoft Office Suite (includes MS Word and Powerpoint)
  • Quicktime Player
  • Safari
  • Stuffit
  • Windows Media Player 9

iDell PCs

On the lower level, there are 3 PCs which are dedicated to searching the Library catalog. As well, there are 8 PCs for general use, one being the workstation for retrieving documents from the scanner. All of the PCs have the following software:

  • Windows XP
  • Internet Explorer
  • Adobe Acrobat Professional
  • Microsoft Office Suite (includes MS Word and Powerpoint)
  • CD burning
  • Adobe Photoshop CS2
  • Windows Media Player

iStudy Tables

All of the study tables on the lower level have pull-down electrical outlets to support laptop computer use.

iLaptop Lounge

In an alcove on the lower level, the Laptop Lounge provides flexible seating for laptop computer use.
